About SafeTrack

The Problem

Corps members are often posted to unfamiliar towns, far from home and support networks. Long road journeys to camp and PPA, unfamiliar neighborhoods, and limited verified housing options leave many corps members exposed during their service year.

Our Solution

SafeTrack keeps corps members connected and protected throughout service — sharing last known location during high-risk situations, flagging threat zones nearby, logging transit details before travel, and helping members find verified housing near their PPA.
